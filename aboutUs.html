FBC Broker

FBC Broker is a relatively young, growing company that provides a wide range of insurance brokerage services. The company was established as part of the change of the current status of a brokerage company existing on the market for 8 years.

FBC Broker is therefore a continuation of the professional knowledge of qualified employees with several years of experience gained both in working for insurance companies and brokerage companies. These people participate in numerous innovative projects carried out on the Polish insurance market.

Thanks to the experience gained and the professional approach to the client, FBC Broker has become a significant entity in the brokerage industry, as evidenced by the constantly growing group of our clients.
